Safety

Ensuring safety is one of the big challenges in fleet management. HOS violation notifications, other Safety alerts, maintaining inspection records, smart Dashcam, etc are few striking features of TXT ELD through which you can achieve compliance standards and avoid critical occurrences and the cost related to it. TXT ELD keeps your employees and assets safe.

Compliance

The fleet regulations are ever-changing, and it is essential to stay compliant. We stay on top of the latest changes so we can keep our clients informed and address any challenges that may arise from new compliance requirements. We help you stay in compliance with all Department of Transportation (DOT), the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA), and the International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) regulations and protect yourself from costly penalties and liabilities.

Efficiency

The vehicle uptime is critical in the asset-intensive transportation industry. Poor maintenance of the vehicle results in unplanned breakdowns, less than optimal performance, and costly repairs. The optimizing maintenance means minimizing the cost of maintenance while maximizing the product uptime. Right Maintenance strategies, processes, and systems can prevent costly failures and minimize downtime. The vehicle visibility can also help in route optimisation.

Telematics

The smart telematics, along with the GPS technology, is the most powerful feature of TXT ELD which gathers measurable, actionable data about the vehicle. By monitoring trip data, vehicle systems information, and driver behaviour, you can identify critical areas of improvement to drive cost savings and improve safety. The device sends, receives and stores information about the vehicle. You can easily monitor emissions, speed and idle times, system diagnostics, and much more.

Asset

Make sure your equipment doesn’t have more downtime by timely maintenance schedules. The predictive maintenance features enable it to happen. It also reduces the high costs caused by the breakdown of the vehicle when the maintenance schedule is ignored. You can manage well by utilizing your vehicle to its maximum.
