Asset

TXT solution helps you with the visibility of your vehicles so; accordingly, you plan and schedule dispatches. The location-based data generation help in identifying if the carrier is in use and enables the fleet managers to take actions. The data generated and maintained get the insights complete service history of the carrier and create alerts on the maintenance schedule. TXT solution keeps you plan your asset maintenance and reduce downtime.

Benefits

Lower Maintenance Cost

The systematic data upkeep for vehicle health by rigorous monitoring helps the fleet managers to bring down the maintenance cost of the vehicles.

Predictive Maintenance

The small maintenance issue if go unnoticed can quickly scale up to a significant and expensive problem. This feature sends alerts to the home office on engine diagnostic data and insights into a machine health condition. TXT solution helps you to keep track of breakdown trends and machine records and predict the requirement of next maintenance.

Reduce Downtime

One of the crucial aspects and objectives of the fleet industry is to maximize vehicle uptime. TXT solution helps you to achieve it by increasing vehicle utilization. The constant data flow of the vehicle movement and the back office is integrated and enhances the coordination to keep the asset on the road for long hours.
