TXT ELD help you to

Integrate And Track Your
Fleet Operations And Bring Safety.

TXT ELD technology is hardwired and directly connected to the Engine Control Unit (ECM) of a carrier and embedded with a Mobile Data Terminal.

It offers complete safety and compliant solution bundle sustaining with its hardware and software application, integrating the essential functions of the fleet companies by generating accurate real-time data for actions. This technology picks up data from the engine and along with the driver's application data, create correct records on vehicle motions, HOS, location information, driving pattern, duty status, system malfunctions, etc. which is reliable.
